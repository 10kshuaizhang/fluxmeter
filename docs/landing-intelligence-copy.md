## Problem

AI margins are opaque. Usage dashboards show *what* ran; invoice tools show *what* was billed. Neither explains why margin dropped last month, which customers lose money, or whether switching models or running a promo pays off. Finance and founders stitch together Grafana, spreadsheets, and billing exports — and still miss the prescriptive answer.

## Solution

FluxMeter Intelligence reads native usage and cost from your metering pipeline, overlays revenue from OpenMeter (or manual import), and returns actionable answers via API:

- **Root cause** — spend delta decomposition with top drivers
- **Unit economics** — revenue vs cost, margin, loss alerts
- **Simulation** — model switch, prompt optimization, token promo what-if
- **Summary** — one headline for the board meeting

Layer 3 metering and guardrails stay in place; Layer 4 intelligence sits on top. Observability and billing platforms remain complementary — overlay ingest, not replace.
